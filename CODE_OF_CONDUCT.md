# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Contributors are expected to be direct, respectful, and technically honest.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment or personal attacks
- doxxing or sharing private information
- discriminatory or abusive language
- deliberate disruption, spam, or bad-faith review behavior

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpreting and enforcing this policy.

Serious or repeated violations may lead to removal of comments, issue restrictions, pull request closure, or bans from participating in the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to the maintainers through a private channel. Do not use public issues for conduct reports that involve personal or sensitive information.
